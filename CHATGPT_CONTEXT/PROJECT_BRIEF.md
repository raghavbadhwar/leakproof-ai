# Project Brief

## Product

LeakProof AI helps B2B companies recover or prevent revenue leakage by comparing contract terms against invoice and usage data.

## Target Users

Primary buyers:

- Founder/CEO
- CFO
- Finance lead
- RevOps lead
- Head of Operations

Primary operators:

- Finance associate
- RevOps manager
- Billing manager
- Customer success operations manager

## Main Workflow

1. Sign in.
2. Create/select organization.
3. Create/select audit workspace.
4. Upload contract, invoice CSV, and usage CSV.
5. Parse and chunk documents.
6. Extract commercial terms with Gemini.
7. Human reviewer approves, edits, rejects, or marks terms for review.
8. Run deterministic reconciliation.
9. Review findings.
10. Attach/approve/reject evidence candidates.
11. Approve findings for customer-ready use.
12. Generate and export customer-ready report.

## What The MVP Finds

- Invoice amount below minimum commitment.
- Unbilled usage overages.
- Unbilled seats.
- Expired discount still applied.
- Missed annual uplift.
- Renewal or notice-window risk.
- Conflicting amendment risk.

## Non-Goals

- No chatbot-first interface.
- No automatic customer email sending.
- No automatic invoice sending.
- No Stripe or payment automation.
- No legal advice.
- No full CLM replacement.
- No broad enterprise integrations before the upload-based workflow is proven.

## Trust Rules

Every money claim must have source evidence, deterministic calculation, and human approval.
