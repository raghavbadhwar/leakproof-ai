# Launch Playbook

## Launch goal

Get 3 paid audit customers before building deep integrations.

## Week 1 — Build and demo

- Finish upload-based MVP.
- Create demo workspace with sample data.
- Record a 3-minute demo video.
- Create a one-page landing page.
- Prepare a simple NDA and data deletion policy.

## Week 2 — Founder-led sales

Contact 50 prospects:

- 20 B2B SaaS founders
- 10 CFOs/finance leads
- 10 RevOps leaders
- 10 agency/MSP owners

Goal: 10 calls, 3 pilots, 1 paid audit.

## Week 3 — Deliver audits manually with software

Use the product, but do manual review before sending results. This helps improve prompts, rules, and UI.

## Week 4 — Convert to subscription

Offer monthly monitoring:

- new contracts reviewed monthly,
- invoice/usage reconciliation monthly,
- renewals and discount expiries monitored,
- evidence packs for finance team.

## Landing page structure

1. Hero: “Find revenue your contracts already earned.”
2. Pain: “Contracts say one thing. Invoices often say another.”
3. How it works: upload, extract, reconcile, recover.
4. Example finding.
5. Security note.
6. Pricing/audit offer.
7. Call to action.

## Proof you need

- A before/after example.
- A finding with citations.
- A calculation that a CFO can verify.
- A statement of what the app does not do.

## What to avoid

- Do not call it a “legal AI” product.
- Do not promise guaranteed recovery.
- Do not ask for large integration projects before proving demand.
- Do not optimize the UI before first paid audit.
