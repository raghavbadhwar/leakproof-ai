# Customer Audit Checklist

Use this checklist during the first paid audits.

## Ask customer for

- 5–20 signed customer contracts
- invoice export for matching customers
- usage or seat export for matching periods
- price book if available
- list of known custom discounts
- renewal calendar if available

## Promise customer

- We will not send anything to their customers.
- We will not change invoices.
- We will produce findings for review.
- We will delete files after audit if requested.

## Deliverables

- summary of total potential leakage
- finding-by-finding evidence packs
- recommended next actions
- prevention recommendations
- suggested monthly monitoring setup

## Questions to ask on sales call

1. Do you have custom pricing or mostly standard pricing?
2. Are contracts and invoices managed in the same system?
3. Do you have usage-based or seat-based charges?
4. Do discounts expire automatically or manually?
5. Who checks annual uplifts and renewals?
6. When was the last contract-to-invoice audit?
