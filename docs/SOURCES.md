## Research conclusion

There are existing CLM, billing, SaaS spend, and procurement tools. The underbuilt wedge is a narrow, upload-first, evidence-backed recovery agent for smaller companies that do not want an enterprise CLM implementation. The first product should sell recovered/prevented leakage, not generic AI automation.
