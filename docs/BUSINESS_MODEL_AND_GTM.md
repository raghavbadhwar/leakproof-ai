# Business Model and Go-To-Market

## Core positioning

LeakProof AI is not a generic AI tool. It is an outcome product for finance teams:

> “We find revenue leakage hiding between your contracts, usage, and invoices.”

## Why buyers pay

The product is tied to measurable money. A buyer does not need to believe in AI. They only need to believe in the evidence pack.

## Initial offer

### Offer 1 — Done-for-you audit

- Price: USD 1,500–3,000 upfront
- Success fee: 5–10% of verified recoverable leakage
- Delivery: 7–14 days
- Requirement: contract samples, invoice exports, usage/seat exports

### Offer 2 — Software subscription after audit

- Starter: USD 399/month for small teams
- Growth: USD 999/month for up to a set number of contracts/customers
- Pro: USD 1,999+/month for more volume, integrations, and workflow approvals

## Why audit-first is better than SaaS-first

Audit-first lets you sell before all integrations are built. It gives you real customer files and reveals the true leakage categories. That data becomes the product roadmap.

## Ideal first customers

- B2B SaaS with custom contracts and usage/seat pricing
- IT services firms with retainers and overage clauses
- digital agencies with scope creep and minimum commitments
- telecom/reseller businesses with complex pricing
- managed service providers with contract renewals and add-ons

## Outreach message

Subject: Quick revenue leakage audit?

```text
Hi {{Name}},

I am building LeakProof AI. It finds under-billing hidden between customer contracts, invoices, and usage data.

This is not a chatbot. The output is an evidence pack showing missed overages, expired discounts, minimum commitments, annual uplifts, or renewal risks.

I am offering a limited audit for B2B companies. If we do not find recoverable or preventable leakage, you do not pay the audit fee.

Worth a 15-minute look?
```

## Expansion path

### Stage 1

Upload-based audits.

### Stage 2

Integrations with billing/accounting/CRM systems.

### Stage 3

Automated monthly leakage monitoring.

### Stage 4

Benchmarking and leakage prevention recommendations.

### Stage 5

D2C personal bill/refund agent.

## Global scaling strategy

- Start with English-language contracts and USD.
- Add multi-currency support early in the data model.
- Add country-specific templates only after demand.
- Expand from B2B SaaS into agencies, telecom, cloud resellers, logistics, and professional services.
