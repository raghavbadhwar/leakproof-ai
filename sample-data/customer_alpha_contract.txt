MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT

Customer: Alpha Retail Cloud Ltd.
Supplier: ExampleSoft Inc.
Effective Date: 2026-01-01
Initial Term: 12 months.

Section 4.1 Fees and Minimum Commitment
Customer will pay a minimum monthly commitment of USD 10,000 for the platform subscription.

Section 4.2 Usage Allowance and Overage
The monthly subscription includes 100,000 API calls per calendar month. Usage above the included allowance will be billed at USD 0.01 per additional API call.

Section 4.3 Annual Uplift
Fees will increase by 5% on each anniversary of the Effective Date.

Section 4.4 Discount
A 20% promotional discount applies only until 2026-03-31. After that date, standard pricing applies.

Section 5 Payment Terms
Invoices are payable net 30 days from invoice date.

Section 8 Renewal
The agreement renews automatically for successive 12-month periods unless either party gives 60 days written notice before the end of the then-current term.
